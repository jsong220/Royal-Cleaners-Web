Organic Cleaning

Traditionally, dry cleaners use a chemical termed PERC to clean customer clothes. While this chemical is effective at cleaning and safe for the customers, it produces dangerous by-products. This can have potential health dangers to store owners who must handle these wastes and has the risk of polluting the ground underneath the machines that use PERC. While there are regulations to minimize pollution, we decided to take the proactive step of upgrading our machines to EM based cleaning. EM based cleaning does not require any usage of PERC and only uses environmental friendly organic solvents to effectively clean clothes like new!
